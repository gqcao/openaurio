More than a chatbot

Vera isn't just an AI — she's a character with depth, personality, and emotional intelligence. She detects your mood, celebrates your wins, and encourages you through challenges.

Built for SFI (Swedish for Immigrants) students, Vera helps you practice real conversations you'll actually have in Sweden — ordering fika, shopping for groceries, finding an apartment.

Why openaurio?

🎭

Character-Driven Learning

Learn through emotional connection with AI characters who have personalities, backstories, and genuine conversations.

🎯

Scenario-Based Practice

Real-world situations: fika with friends, grocery shopping, apartment hunting. Practice what you'll actually use.

😊

Mood Detection

Vera senses when you're tired, frustrated, or excited — and adapts her teaching style to match your energy.

📈

Progress Tracking

XP points, levels (A1→B2), daily streaks, and achievements. See your Swedish improve over time.

🧠

Long-Term Memory

Vera remembers your name, Swedish level, personal details, and past conversations. Build a real relationship.

🇸🇪

Built for SFI

Designed for Swedish for Immigrants students. Aligned with SFI curriculum levels A-D.
